    Our Values

    We have four cardinal principles we work by and they are:

    Transparency
    We won't promise not to throw around technical jargons, but when and if we do, we'll be sure to bring you up to speed. Also, considering we're mostly students with limited experience, it's to your benefit and ours that we're upfront with you when we don't know and we'll do just that when it comes to it.
    Efficiency
    Time is of the essence for every business[wo]man and so we'll be prompt to meet set dealines and milestones for every project we embark on to make sure you're satisfied. However, we will not sacrifice efficiency of your solution simply to "get it done".
    Quality
    Opensource software is notorious for having bugs and also being a tad more difficult to omplement than their proprietary counterpart. They also have a habit of having terrible UIs. We won't let that stop us from giving you the best they have to offer
    Reliability
    Being available when you need us to be and supporting you whenever you need it. Offering robust and solutions and not using the fact that "it's opensource" an excuse to deliver sub-par service(s). We want to earn your trust and your respect as an individual and as a business.
