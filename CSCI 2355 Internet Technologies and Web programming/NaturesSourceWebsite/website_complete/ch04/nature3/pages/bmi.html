Your Health: Compute Your BMI

Coming soon ... a BMI (Body Mass Index) calculator. The first tool we will provide will be a calculator our clients can use to find their Body Mass Index, a numerical quantity that can be used to give some indication of whether a person's weight lies within a normal range. With the tendency to obesity among the general population these days, this should be a useful tool.
